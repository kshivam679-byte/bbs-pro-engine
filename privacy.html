Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Welcome to BBS Pro Engine. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy document explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our web application or mobile APK.

1. Information We Collect

Our core application (BBS Engine, Calculators, Estimators) operates entirely inside your local device context environment. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your structural engineering data, project files, or calculation metrics to any external servers. All project history and sheets are stored safely on your device's local storage.

2. Third-Party Services & SDKs

To provide a complete mobile app experience, our Android/Amazon wrapper integrates third-party services which may collect information used to identify your device:

  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, location data, or other diagnostic data to personalize ads.
  • OneSignal: Used to send push notifications regarding app updates or utility tools. OneSignal may collect device tokens and network state data.
  • Google Play Services: Used for core app functionalities and stability tracking.

3. Data Security

Since your calculation data is processed entirely in your client browser/webview context environment, it is highly secure. However, please note that clearing your mobile browser cache or app data may delete your locally saved backups.

4. Children's Privacy

Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective immediately when posted on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us through our official GitHub repository channel.
